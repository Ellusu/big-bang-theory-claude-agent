```markdown
1. Introduction
2. Data Controller (company, address, DPO)
3. What Data We Collect (user-provided, automatic, third-party)
4. Legal Basis (contract, legal obligation, legitimate interest, consent)
5. How We Use Data
6. Data Sharing (service providers, legal authorities)
7. Data Retention (timelines per data type)
8. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15-21: access, rectification, erasure, etc.)
9. Cookies (see Cookie Policy)
10. International Transfers (SCCs if applicable)
11. Data Security (TLS, access controls, audits)
12. Children's Privacy (18+ only for alcohol)
13. Changes to Policy
14. Contact & Complaints (DPO, supervisory authority)
```

### Cookie Policy Structure

## GDPR Compliance

### Legal Basis (Art. 6)
- **Contract performance** (Art. 6(1)(b)): Provide service, process orders
- **Legal obligation** (Art. 6(1)(c)): Tax, anti-money laundering
- **Legitimate interest** (Art. 6(1)(f)): Fraud prevention, analytics
- **Consent** (Art. 6(1)(a)): Marketing, non-essential cookies

### User Rights
- **Access** (Art. 15): Export personal data
- **Rectification** (Art. 16): Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** (Art. 17): "Right to be forgotten"
- **Restrict processing** (Art. 18): Limit data use
- **Data portability** (Art. 20): Export in machine-readable format
- **Object** (Art. 21): Stop processing for marketing

### Data Retention
```
Account data: Duration + 5 years (tax law)
Order history: 10 years (commercial law)
Marketing consent: Until withdrawn
Logs: 12 months
```

## Incident Response

### Data Breach (GDPR Art. 33-34)
**Within 72 hours:**

1. **Assess**: Type of data, number affected, consequences
2. **Notify supervisory authority**: If risk to rights/freedoms
3. **Notify individuals**: If high risk
4. **Document**: Facts, effects, remedial action
